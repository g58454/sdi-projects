Project 2

The story of a new born spore-ling is a representation of a project that

I have been wanting to create for sometime. He is allowed to choose

wither or not he wants to explore the new world, find out the purpose of

life, or do nothing. If he stays put and explores he can play with

surroundings, and he can leave to go find his purpose. If he chooses to

find his purpose, he can explore, discover, and grow. Lastly, he can

choose to do nothing.

November 7 2013, Aaron Shannon
